The Journey.

Karan started his fitness journey as a competitive powerlifter, obsessed with the mechanics of the human body under heavy loads. But chasing personal records wasn't enough; he wanted to decode the science of building muscle and shedding fat for anyone, regardless of their starting point.

Over the past 8 years, he has completely dismantled old-school bro-science, developing a methodology that relies on evidence-based training and strict nutritional tracking. He built MRFIT on the philosophy that there are no shortcuts—only consistent effort driven by intelligent programming.

His coaching style is intense but precise. Karan expects 100% effort when you step onto the floor. If you bring the dedication, he maps out the blueprint for success.
